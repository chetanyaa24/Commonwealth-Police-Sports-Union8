On India's 76th Republic Day, celebrations reached new heights with the virtual launch of the Commonwealth Police Sports Union website. This momentous occasion was graced by the esteemed presence of Shri Somesh Goyal, a distinguished IPS officer, who inaugurated the website on Google Meet.

The role of sports in fostering esprit de corps and leadership qualities is well recognised. Recognising the contribution of sports in shaping him, the Duke of Wellington, after the victory of his forces in the battle of Waterloo, is chronicled to have remarked that “the battle of Waterloo was won at the field of Eton “ The Commonwealth Police Sports Union is committed to fostering the culture of sports and camaraderie amongst the police personnel of the Commonwealth countries.
